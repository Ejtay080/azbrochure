Within the borders of Sun City AZ there are numerous medical doctors, dentists, eye, hearing and other specialists, and a top-rated medical center. Golf carts provide residents with transportation to reach local healthcare services. Located in the middle of Sun City AZ is the Banner Boswell Medical Center where Sun City’s residents have received outstanding patient care since 1970. More than 700 physicians provide a full spectrum of medical services. This welcoming, supportive environment encourages family and friends to participate in the treatment and healing process.Primary specialty areas at Banner Boswell include cardiology, neurosciences, orthopedic, rehabilitation, and oncology services. Banner Boswell is a designated NICHE hospital through the Hartford Institute for Geriatric Nursing. The NICHE (Nurses Improving Care for Health System Elders) designation means that Banner Boswell has met the criteria for providing optimal care to the geriatric population. Banner Boswell became a nationally-certified Joint Commission Primary Stroke Center in 2010 and has been designated a Community Hospital Comprehensive Cancer Program by the American College of Surgeons Commission on Cancer since 1987. Banner Boswell’s Medical Imagining department holds the American College of Radiology accreditation for CT, MRI, and Ultrasound.
